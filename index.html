Our values

We're not your average
eLearning agency

User-centric design

We love learners, and believe they should be respected. We will spend time understanding your audience so that we can design a solution that actually works within their time and environmental constraints.

Outcomes over outputs

We'll work with you to determine objectives for your learning initiatives and focus on ensuring these get met, rather than just producing some flashy assets because it feels good.

No death by PowerPoint

"Tell me, and I'll forget. Show me, and I may remember. Involve me, and I'll understand." This is our mantra. We focus on interactivity, and allowing users to practise the skills they need in the real world.

Honesty

Not every learning solution is a good idea. If we think there's a better way, we will tell you. Even if it means the project runs shorter, we'll ideate with you to design the best approach.

No patronising

Cartoons belong in children's books, not in professional learning. If someone feels patronised, they'll shut down and won't engage with the content. We prioritise design that creates a meaningful connection.

Collaborative creation

We like to work in a way where everyone's opinion is valid, and we believe that a balanced discussion is what creates the best outcomes. As the saying goes: teamwork makes the dream work.
